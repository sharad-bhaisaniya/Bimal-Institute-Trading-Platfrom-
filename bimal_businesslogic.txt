# Bimal Institute - Mobile App Business Logic & Feature Breakdown

## 1. Core Concept & Vision
**App Name:** Bimal Institute
**Tagline:** "Building Traders, Not Just Students"
**Niche:** Financial Markets, Trading Education, and Live Market Tools.

The app is an ecosystem that transitions a user from a "learner" to a "professional trader". It uses a strict **Role-Based Architecture** to alter the user experience based on their expertise and goals.

## 2. Role Separation Logic (The Heart of the App)
The app dynamically changes its UI and features based on the user's role: `student` or `trader`. 

### A. Student Role (Learning Phase)
- **Focus:** Education, training, course completion, and basic trade logging.
- **Navigation Tabs:** Dashboard | Learn | Journal | AI Coach | Profile
- **Key Features Available:**
  - **Learning Progress:** Track course module completion (e.g., "Advanced Price Action").
  - **Live Classes:** Alerts for upcoming live sessions (e.g., "Options Greek - Batch Alpha").
  - **Student Stats:** Attendance %, Batch Rank, Win Rate, Monthly PnL.

### B. Trader Role (Professional Phase)
- **Focus:** Live market execution, advanced tools, and performance tracking (Education modules are hidden).
- **Navigation Tabs:** Dashboard | Journal | Profile (with future scope for Live Markets/Signals)
- **Key Features Available:**
  - **Distraction-Free UI:** Courses and student metrics are completely hidden.
  - **Advanced Stats:** Focus heavily on Win Rate and Monthly PnL.
  - **Market Updates:** Promo banners for Bootcamps (e.g., Crypto & Forex Mastery) and Live Signals.

## 3. Core App Modules

### 1. Dashboard (The Hub)
- Personalized welcome (e.g., "Welcome back, [Name]").
- Real-time stat cards (Win Rate, PnL, Attendance).
- Promotional Banners (managed dynamically, e.g., Bootcamp registrations).
- Quick glimpse of Recent Trades and upcoming Live Classes.

### 2. Trade Journal (Crucial Feature)
- Users log their daily trades.
- Captures details: Instrument (e.g., BANKNIFTY 45000 CE), Position (Long/Short), PnL, Execution Time, Risk-Reward (RR) ratio, and outcome (Hit Stoploss/Target).
- Used to calculate overall Win Rate and Monthly PnL.

### 3. AI Coach (Automated Mentor)
- Scans the user's Trade Journal to provide behavioral insights.
- Example alert: *"Risk Management Alert - You've hit your max loss limit in 2 of your last 5 trades. Consider reducing your position sizing."*

### 4. Learn & Live Classes
- Video courses structured by modules.
- Scheduled Live Classes (Webinars) specific to batches.

### 5. Profile & Settings
- Manages User KYC (critical for financial apps).
- Learning preferences, subscriptions, payments, and security settings.

## 4. Admin Panel Requirements (What you need to build)
Based on the mobile app's logic, your Admin Panel must have the following modules:

### 1. User & Role Management
- View all registered users.
- **Crucial:** Ability to change a user's role (`student` <-> `trader`). 
- View individual user performance (Win Rate, PnL, Journal entries).
- **KYC Verification System:** Approve or reject submitted KYC documents.

### 2. Course & Content Management (LMS)
- **Course Builder:** Create courses, add modules, upload videos/materials.
- **Live Classes:** Schedule live sessions, assign them to specific "Batches", and push links/alerts to the Student Dashboard.

### 3. Events & Banners Management
- **Promo Banners:** Upload images and set redirect links for the Dashboard banners (e.g., "Crypto Bootcamp").
- Manage event registrations.

### 4. Financials & Subscriptions
- Track user payments, active subscriptions, and subscription tiers.
- Manage access limits (e.g., denying access if payment fails).

### 5. Push Notifications & Signals (For Traders)
- Send custom notifications.
- Push live market updates or trade signals specifically targeting the `trader` user base.

### 6. AI & Analytics Dashboard
- View aggregated data: How many users are in profit/loss?
- Most traded instruments.
- (Optional) Configure the rules for the AI Coach alerts.
